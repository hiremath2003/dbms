Subject: Database Management System

Academic Year: 2025 – 26

Course Activity: Database Design & Normalization

Project Description

The Online Car Rental System automates traditional car rental operations by offering a centralized digital platform. Customers can search, book, and pay for cars online, while administrators manage vehicles, bookings, and payments efficiently.

This system minimizes paperwork, avoids manual errors, ensures data integrity, and enhances customer satisfaction through secure operations.

Project Objectives

Online Car Booking for Rental Service
Automated Car Availability Management
Secure Digital Payment System
